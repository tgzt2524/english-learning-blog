About This Blog

Why This Blog?

This blog is a personal space for documenting my English learning journey. I believe the best way to learn is to share — writing helps consolidate knowledge, and sharing opens the door to feedback and discussion.

What You'll Find Here

  • Grammar tips — Common pitfalls and explanations
  • Vocabulary building — Word lists, collocations, idioms
  • Reading notes — Insights from articles and books
  • Writing practice — Essays and corrections
  • Learning resources — Tools, websites, and methods

How to Contribute

Found a mistake or want to share your own insights? This blog is open source! Feel free to open an issue or submit a pull request on GitHub.
